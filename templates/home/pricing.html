More about our services

Web development

At pixenweb one of our core role is to develop a fully functioning website to meet your needs. We believe that you get full control of anything if you fully understand its base. Therefore, we develop websites from scratch using the latest technologies. This enables us have full control and proper understanding of your website such that if anything goes wrong or a bug is spotted we are able to find it easily, fix it and restart your site as quickly as possible as we don't have to consult any third party.

Responsive design

It is our responsibility at pixenweb to make your website viewable not only in desktop screens but also in small screen devices like smartphones and tablets. Most people currently are using smartphones to access the internet. Therefore,we deploy all mobile responsive measures and technologies to make sure your website is viewable on all screen sizes that are used on the planet earth.

Web maintenance

Like cars need regular repair and check-up, the same applies to websites. As a website owner you may want to change,add or remove a feature in your website. At pixenweb, we offer maintenance and updates for you in your website to make sure everything is as you want it to be and functions as you expect.

Mobile application

Having a website is an investment. Having a mobile app for your website is double investment. Imagine this,someone visits your websites on a browser, after closing the browser they may forget about you.This is normal, but you want to maintain the client. With an app that your clients can download in their phones, the probability of the clients forgetting you is minimized as they will be viewing your app every time they switch on their mobile phones and remember you. At pixenweb, we help create this long term contact with your clients by creating mobile application for your website that is downloadable on every mobile platform.
